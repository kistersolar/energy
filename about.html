Who We Are

Founded in 2015, Kister Solar Energy began as a small startup with a vision to bring reliable and affordable solar power solutions to Nigerian homes and businesses. What started as a two-person operation has now grown into one of the most trusted names in Nigeria's renewable energy sector.

Our Journey

In our first year, we installed solar systems for just 15 homes in Lagos. Today, we've powered over 5,000 homes and businesses across 12 states in Nigeria. Our growth has been fueled by our commitment to quality products and exceptional customer service.

Milestones

  • 2015: Company founded with initial focus on Lagos market
  • 2017: Expanded operations to 3 additional states
  • 2019: Launched our own line of rechargeable solar fans
  • 2021: Recognized as "Solar Company of the Year" by Renewable Energy Association of Nigeria
  • 2023: Reached 5,000+ satisfied customers nationwide

Our Mission

To provide affordable, reliable, and sustainable solar energy solutions that power Nigerian homes and businesses while reducing environmental impact. We believe in energy solutions that work for Africa's unique climate and needs.
